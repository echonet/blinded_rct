Blinded, randomized trial of sonographer versus AI cardiac function assessment
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This repository contains study protocol, statistical analysis plan, and de-identified trial results for

> [**Blinded, randomized trial of sonographer versus AI cardiac function assessment**](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-023-05947-3)<br/>
  Bryan He, Alan C. Kwan, Jae Hyung Cho, Neal Yuan, Charles Pollick, Takahiro Shiota, Joseph Ebinger, Natalie A. Bello, Janet Wei, Kiranbir Josan, Grant Duffy, Melvin Jujjavarapu, Robert Siegel, Susan Cheng, James Y. Zou and David Ouyang. <b>Nature</b>, April 5, 2023. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-023-05947-3

For more details on the model and training data, see the accompanying paper,

> [**Video-based AI for beat-to-beat assessment of cardiac function**](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-020-2145-8)<br/>
  David Ouyang, Bryan He, Amirata Ghorbani, Neal Yuan, Joseph Ebinger, Curt P. Langlotz, Paul A. Heidenreich, Robert A. Harrington, David H. Liang, Euan A. Ashley, and James Y. Zou. <b>Nature</b>, March 25, 2020. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41586-020-2145-8

  and repository at https://github.com/echonet/dynamic.
